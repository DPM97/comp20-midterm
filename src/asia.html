Asia

Airfare

With the LLTD Asia package, you'll fly direct to Tokyo on All Nipon Airways. This one week package includes the very best of Japan's largest city.

Lodging

Relax in the elegance of a traditional ryokan lodging. Don a kimono and sit in peaceful contemplation before a Japanese rock garden..

Attractions

In Tokyo explore explore the modern metropolis of Akihabara, home of anime and video games. Visit traditional Asakusa temple and take a trip back in time at the Edo-Tokyo Museum. Ride the bustling Metro, and ascend to the top of Tokyo Sky Tree.

Dining

Sample the best Japanese cuisine: learn how to use chopsticks, enjoy a traditional tea ceremony, eat at a conveyor belt sushi restaurant, and eat at a world-class ramen bar.
